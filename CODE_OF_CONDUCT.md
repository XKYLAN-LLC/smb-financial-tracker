# Code Of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want this project to be useful, practical, and welcoming for people building tools around sensitive financial workflows.

Contributors are expected to communicate respectfully, assume good intent, and handle privacy-sensitive topics with care.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and direct.
- Keep feedback focused on the work.
- Treat user privacy as a first-class concern.
- Use synthetic examples in public discussions.
- Flag uncertainty instead of overstating financial, tax, legal, benefits, or insurance conclusions.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private user data.
- Encouraging fraudulent tax, benefits, insurance, or financial behavior.
- Presenting professional determinations as authoritative when they require qualified review.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, reject pull requests, or block contributors who violate this code of conduct.
